# 05-grants-and-money/

**Purpose:** Parallel income and runway extension. Hackathon is one pot; Superteam Earn bounties + Foundation/ecosystem grants are a second and third.

## Files
- `superteam-earn-bounties.md` — [Day 2] open bounties worth >$500 that align with current thesis direction
- `superteam-grants.md` — [Day 3] Superteam builder grants process + check size
- `solana-foundation-grants.md` — [Day 3] Foundation grant tiers + typical recipients
- `ecosystem-grants.md` — [Day 3] Helius / Jito / Jupiter / Squads partner programs

## Strategy
- Don't chase every grant. Pick the 1–2 whose thesis-shape overlaps with the hackathon submission.
- Many hackathon winners get fast-tracked for Foundation grants — treat the hackathon as the primary filter.
