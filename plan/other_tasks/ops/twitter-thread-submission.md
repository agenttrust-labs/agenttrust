## What to do if submission upload fails Day 17

If Colosseum's submission portal rejects upload (file size, server, etc.):

- **Hold the thread.** Don't post "submitted" tweet until submission is actually in.
- Email submission to `hackathon@colosseum.com` per Frontier rules.
- Once email confirmation arrives, post Tweet 1 with " — submission via email backup, Colosseum confirmed" parenthetical.

**Don't claim shipped if not shipped.** Matty + Mert both publicly hate over-claiming.
